I think there's no question that there are rewards for cheating.  I don't think we're very good at looking clearly at what that means.  Our blessed governments set up the current academic system to have greater competition and more explicit rewards.  Doing this has a predictable effect on the quality of work and commitment to the work.   We can't fix it until we return to the basis of what science is for and why it is worth doing.
